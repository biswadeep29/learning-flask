About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world. Based in Madrid, Spain, the club has a rich history of dominance in both domestic and international competitions. With a record number of La Liga and UEFA Champions League titles, Real Madrid is known for its attacking football, legendary players like Cristiano Ronaldo, Zinedine Zidane, and Alfredo Di Stéfano, and its fierce rivalry with FC Barcelona in El Clásico. Playing at the iconic Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, the club continues to be a global powerhouse, attracting millions of passionate fans worldwide.
